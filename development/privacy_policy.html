CalNutriPal Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the CalNutriPal mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

When you use our App, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: The App collects information that you voluntarily provide, such as height, weight, age, and activity level for calculating nutritional goals.
  • Nutrition Data: Food items, meals, and nutritional information that you log in the App.
  • User Preferences: Settings and preferences such as unit system (metric/imperial) and notification preferences.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the App, including session duration, features used, and interactions with the App's interface.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App functionality
  • Calculate appropriate nutritional goals based on your provided data
  • Track your progress toward your nutrition and fitness goals
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the user experience
  • Debug and fix technical issues

Data Storage and Security

Your personal information and nutrition data are primarily stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data.

Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics that help us improve the App. This includes information such as:

  • App opens and usage duration
  • Which features are used most frequently
  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • General location information (country level only)

Firebase Analytics may collect and process certain device identifiers such as Advertising ID to provide its services. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information about how Firebase processes data.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymous, aggregated data with partners or in public reports.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data from our systems
  • Opt out of analytics collection

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new privacy policy in the App.
